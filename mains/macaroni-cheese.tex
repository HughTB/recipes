\recipe{Macaroni Cheese}{01:00}{Vegetarian}{3}{2}{
  \item 500ml Milk
  \item 250g Macaroni or Other Pasta
  \item 150g Cheddar Cheese
  \item 25g Parmesean Cheese
  \item 25g Cornflour
}{
  \item If grilling, heat an oven to 200$^{\circ}$C/ 180$^{\circ}$C Fan/ Gas Mark 6
  \item Mix 3tbsp of milk with the cornflour and heat the remaining milk on a medium heat
  \item Start the pasta in another pan, cook for recommended time or until al-dente
  \item Mix the cornflour slurry into the rest of the milk and keep stirring over a low heat until it thickens
  \item Remove the sauce mix from the heat and stir in most of the cheddar and parmesean
  \item Drain the pasta well then stir into the sauce, in an oven-proof dish
  \item Sprinkle the remaining cheddar over the top, then bake for 15-20 minutes or until the sauce browns on top
}
